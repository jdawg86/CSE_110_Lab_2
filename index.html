Such as doge and trollface the depression of 2020 and overall negative outlook towards the future have resulted in some rather twisted memes of once light-hearted characters.

Modern Doge

However, now Doge has evolved into a whole host of dog characters to represent different parts of society

Doge has become a symbol of the internet as in 2020, Doge was voted MEME OF THE DECADE

Cheemsburbger (see image above) is one of the most beloved doge spinoffs. Cheems is a "doge" with a speech impediment and despite his struggles with bullying, he lived a great life

He found and married a girl he loved and became an astronaut. Yet despite his success, his dream is what eventually led to his demise.
